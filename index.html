COHERENCE v1.0

A cyberpunk roguelike where you play as an uploaded mind navigating crumbling virtual ship systems.

Built for 7DRL 2026.

controls

WASD / Arrows — move

Enter — transfer at interface exit

Click — move to tile / open terminal

F — toggle aim mode (corrupt.m)

RMB — quick-fire at nearest visible target

F / LMB — fire at target (in aim mode)

Esc — cancel aim mode

combat

corrupt.m: Right-click to instantly fire at the nearest visible enemy (range: 8, line of sight). Or press F to enter aim mode and pick your target manually with click or F.

Melee: walk into an enemy to strike.
