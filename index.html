COHERENCE

A cyberpunk roguelike where you play as an uploaded mind navigating crumbling virtual ship systems.

Built for 7DRL 2026.

controls

WASD / Arrows — move

Enter — transfer at interface exit

E — interact with adjacent terminal / echo

Click — move to tile / open terminal

F — toggle aim mode

F / RMB — fire at target (in aim mode)

Esc — cancel aim mode

~ — toggle debug panel

combat

Enter aim mode with F, then click or press F on a visible target to corrupt-shoot it (range: 8 tiles, requires clear line of sight).

Melee: walk into an enemy to strike.
