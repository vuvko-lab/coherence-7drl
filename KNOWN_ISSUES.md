# Known bugs and issues

## UI

* At least one report of locked door shown with a unlocked door glyph.

## Map generation

* Placed entities from special events can block the path to a door.
* "Key" placement for deactivating rooms can be placed such that they both cannot be retrieved.
* I encountered at least one time when I could not open a quarantine room at cluster 4. Maybe check the quarantine room placement for clusters 4+.

## Entity behavior

* After some time AI just stops (happened at turn 2000+).
