Coherence

An uploaded mind in a dying virtual ship. Can you restore coherence before it's too late?

You are an uploaded mind—a ship engineer booted from a damaged backup into the crumbling virtual systems of the vessel you once called home. The ship is tearing itself apart as anti-virus programs wage war against remnants of a hostile breach.

Navigate through clusters of decaying server rooms, each a distinct zone of the ship's infrastructure. Stealth or fight your way to the root console. Hack terminals to unlock the interface exits. Restore the ship's controls before the war destroys everything.

Your coherence is your life force. Damage to your mind is permanent. Choose your actions wisely—each hack, each combat, each corrupt operation brings you closer to the console... or to deletion.

Features

  • Procedural clusters - Each run generates unique room layouts with interconnected corridors
  • Module system - Equip and manage software modules: alert, overclock, corrupt, cloak, spoof
  • Nine hazard types - Corrupted zones, trigger traps, memory leaks, firewalls, and more
  • Four enemy factions - Chroniclers, Bit-Mites, Logic Leeches, and White-Hats with distinct AI
  • Speed-based turns - Strategic positioning matters; faster entities act more frequently
  • Terminal hacking - Find and activate terminals to unlock cluster exits
  • Seed support - Share seeds to reproduce specific runs

Gameplay

Move through a 50×30 grid of procedurally generated rooms. Each cluster is a self-contained section of the ship's virtual infrastructure, connected to others via interface exits (⇋). Exit paths are locked until you activate the terminal in each cluster.

Your alert.m module actively scans for nearby threats. Use corrupt.m to brute-force obstacles but beware—the more damage you cause, the more the ship's anti-virus treats you as a threat. cloak.m lets you sneak past enemies. overclock.m grants extra speed in critical moments.

Hazards

  • Corrupted zones
  • Trigger traps
  • Memory leaks
  • Firewalls
  • Unstable sectors
  • Quarantine areas
  • Echo chambers
  • Gravity wells
  • Cascade failures

Enemies

  • Chroniclers - Wander and catalog; broadcast your position
  • Bit-Mites - Chase and attack on sight
  • Logic Leeches - Wall-walk, stalk, then charge
  • White-Hats - Patrol routes, chase and attack

Controls

  • W A S D or ↑ ↓ ← → — Move
  • Enter — Transfer to next/previous cluster
  • Click adjacent tile — Move immediately
  • Click distant tile — Auto-walk to target
